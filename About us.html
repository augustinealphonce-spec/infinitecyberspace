About Us

Welcome to the Kisumu Cybersecurity Hub, proudly founded in 2024 by Augustine Alphonse Ouma.

Born from a bold vision, we are ready to redefine Kisumu’s digital future — an innovation hub where SMEs, entrepreneurs, and individuals connect to technology, cyberspace, and the digital economy. Here, ideas turn into opportunities, and readiness meets innovation to drive growth across Kisumu County and beyond.

Our Mission

To secure, connect, and empower Kisumu’s digital future by providing modern cybersecurity solutions, hands-on training, mentorship, and awareness programs that help businesses and individuals thrive online.

Our Vision

To create a safe, inclusive digital environment — an innovation hub that connects SMEs, entrepreneurs, and individuals in Kisumu County and beyond to technology, cybersecurity, and online business opportunities.

Our Values

We believe that protection is the foundation of trust. That’s why we focus on safeguarding sensitive data, preventing cyber threats, and building resilience in the digital economy.

Beyond security, we are committed to unlocking potential. By partnering with SMEs and guiding young talent, we nurture digital skills that bring out the best in every individual. This approach not only equips businesses with capable professionals but also opens doors to new opportunities in cyberspace, the digital economy, and innovation.

Our Impact

Kisumu Cyberspace Hub is laying the foundation to train SMEs, empower youth with IT skills, and build partnerships that will strengthen Kisumu’s digital resilience. Every solution we plan is also a step toward addressing unemployment challenges in our community.
