About Me

I’m Augustine Ouma, Founder and CEO of Infinite Cyberspace Hub — a forward-thinking cybersecurity and IT solutions company based in Kisumu County, Kenya.

With a strong background in Information Technology and Cybersecurity, I’m passionate about protecting businesses from digital threats while empowering them with innovative tools to thrive in the modern economy.

Vision

To build a secure and trusted digital ecosystem where Kenyan SMEs can grow confidently in the face of evolving cyber threats.

Mission

Deliver world-class cybersecurity and IT solutions, promote digital literacy, and mentor the next generation of tech leaders across East Africa.

Core Values

  • Innovation – Staying ahead of cyber threats
  • Integrity – Building lasting trust with clients
  • Empowerment – Growing local talent and businesses
  • Excellence – Delivering reliable, high-quality service
